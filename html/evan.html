About Me

Hi, I'm Evan Liberto. I was born and raised in St. Louis, Missouri. I graduated from Christian Brothers College High School in 2022, where I was a member of the National Honors Society, captain of the varsity swim team and water polo teams, and actively involved on the racquetball team.

I am currently a junior at Miami University pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science. Starting this spring, I will also begin working toward a Master's in Computer Science through Miami's Combined Degree Program. My decision to pursue Computer Science stems from my love for technology and my desire to understand how it influences our daily lives. I've always enjoyed a good challenge and thrive on putting in hard work and effort to achieve my goals. To me, Computer Science offers the perfect combination of problem-solving, creativity, and continuous learning. I'm excited to continue exploring this field and I look forward to discovering what the future holds for me.

At Miami, I've continued my passion for swimming as an active member of the Miami University Club Swim Team, where I currently hold nine team records. Outside of the pool, I enjoy working out, golfing, rock climbing, and mountain biking.

Experience

Through this experience, I gained a deeper understanding of the software development lifecycle, learned the importance of Agile practices and effective communication, and enhanced my skills in API integration and automation.
