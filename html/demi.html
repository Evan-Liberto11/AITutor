About Me

Ever since I was a kid, I've always been more on the math and science side. These interests ended up leading me to the computer science field. My Experience

This past semester and summer, I worked with the federal contracting company Modern Technology Solutions Inc. (MTSI), located in Dayton, Ohio, near the Wright-Patterson Air Force Base. During my internship, I significantly enhanced my knowledge in programming techniques, team collaboration, Agile methodologies, and simulation concepts, while also honing my problem-solving abilities and understanding of professional development workflows.

I contributed to the development of cockpit simulations using the MIXR (Mixed Reality Simulation Platform) framework and conducted data analysis with the AFSIM (Advanced Framework for Simulation, Integration, and Modeling) framework. Additionally, I utilized Git for version control, ensuring efficient team collaboration and project management.
